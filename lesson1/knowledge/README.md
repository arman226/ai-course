### Logical Connectives Symbols

1. **AND** (Conjunction)

   - Symbol: ∧

2. **OR** (Disjunction)

   - Symbol: ∨

3. **NOT** (Negation)

   - Symbol: ¬

4. **IMPLIES** (Implication)

   - Symbol: →

5. **IF AND ONLY IF** (Biconditional)

   - Symbol: ↔

6. **NAND** (Negated AND)

   - Symbol: ↑

7. **NOR** (Negated OR)
   - Symbol: ↓
